Ties for Change About Our Brand

We believe that a business should create value for everyone. Not only do you get a unique, handmade tie, but you help create jobs and build infrastructure for struggling Ghanaians.

Every dollar you spend on our ties goes toward manufacturers in Ghana. We've built our entire business model with this strategic focus in mind, and we live true to it.
